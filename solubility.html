Salts of Akali Metals are soluble

e.g. If M = Li, Na, or K, then MX, MX2, MX3, etc. are soluable regardless of what X is

Ammonium salts (NH4+) are soluble

e.g. NH4X, (NH4)2X, (NH4)3X, etc. are soluable regardless of what X is
